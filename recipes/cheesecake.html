Cheesecake Recipe

A slice of a classic New York style cheesecake

A classic that we all enjoy around the holidays, or with this recipe, any time you feel like it! This is a recipe for a New York style cheesecake, suitable for a variety of mix-ins, or toppings. Included is a recipe for a homemade graham cracker crust, but it can be easily substitued with your own preffered crust.

Cheesecake batter

  • 32 oz cream cheese(room temperature)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 T Vanilla extract

Crust

  • 1 1/4 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 3/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup white sugar

Batter

  1. Beat cream cheese until smooth with mixer paddle attachment,
  2. Add in sugar and beat until smooth
  3. Add eggs one at a time, thouroughly mixing and scraping down bowl between eggs.
  4. Add in vanilla with last egg
  5. Thouroughly fold in sour cream

Crust

  1. Mix melted butter, graham cracker crumbs, and sugar together.
  2. Take crust mixture and pack into cake or springform pan

Baking

  1. Once crust and batter are in cake pan, place into deep roasting pan or casserole dish.
  2. Place deep pan into oven, and fill halfway with hot water.
  3. Place in oven at 325 degrees for half an hour
  4. Lower temp to 275 and bake for another hour, or until done
  5. Cheesecake is done once there is 2-3 inches of jiggle across the center
  6. Let sit on counter for another half hour, then place in refrigerator.
  7. Once cool, slice and enjoy!
