It's Gravy

A pot of gravy. No you can't drink it, fatty.

Biscuits and... C'mon I shouldn't have to tell this. Put away the packet and step into the light, where your breakfast and mashed potatoes no longer suck.

Ingredients

  • Milk, duh. 32 oz
  • 1/4 C Flour
  • 1/4 C butter
  • salt
  • black pepper

The Process

  1. Add your butter into a hot pot and wait until it's melted
  2. Add flour to your hot butter and cook, stirring frequently. Not too frequently though, sort of like pushing around something questionable floating in a pond. This is called a roux, welcome to cooking. It's done when its darkened in color and smells sort of like popcorn.
  3. Add in your milk and whisk it like it owes you money
  4. Add in an appropriate amount of salt and pepper, maybe not at the same time if you're a "seperate but equal" kind of person
  5. Stir frequently on med-high heat until it thickens
  6. We're done here, why are you still reading? Why am I still typing? Is this even real life?
